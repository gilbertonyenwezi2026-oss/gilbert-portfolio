# About Me

I am a supply chain and operations leader with extensive experience across manufacturing, logistics, quality, and distribution. My career has been built on driving operational excellence through data, process improvement, and cross-functional collaboration.

Over the years, I have led initiatives focused on improving efficiency, reducing costs, enhancing quality, and aligning operations with strategic business objectives. My work has consistently centered on translating data into actionable insights that drive measurable performance improvements.

I am currently pursuing a Master’s in Data Science with an Artificial Intelligence specialization at Northwestern University. Through this program, I am expanding my technical capabilities in data science, data engineering, SQL, Python, and Go.

My goal is to operate at the intersection of **operations, analytics, and technology**—building systems that not only generate insights but also enable execution, decision-making, and sustained business impact.
